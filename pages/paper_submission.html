Paper submission

Submissions to ASAP 2018 must use the double-column IEEE conference proceedings format.

ASAP 2018 will accept 8-page full papers for oral presentations (up to 1 extra pages can be bought), 4-page short papers for short oral or poster presentations, with a double-blind review process. Manuscripts must not identify authors or their affiliations. Papers are to be submitted through EasyChair

All accepted submissions must be presented at the conference. Failure to present will result in the removal of the submission from the proceedings before publication in IEEE Xplore.
